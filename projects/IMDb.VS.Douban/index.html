About the genre, the most popular genre from both websites is "Drama". Besides that, the IMDb ranking seems to be preferring "Action", "Crime", "Adventure" films, while the proportion of those from Douban website is relatively low.

One interesting fact is that there is no films with the genre "Family" in the top 50 films of IMDb, while on Douban, there are a few. There could be several reasons behind this interesting fact. One can be the western, especially US film industry puts more focus on the action films, and since IMDb users are mainly target audience of the US market, this may cause the top 50 films contains action films the most. The other reason might be that Chinese people have the culture of watching films together as a family, leading to the high view rate of those family films, which are good and appropriate to watch together with family. And most family movies are touching, making the rate easier to get high.
